Shepherds Pie

Gordon Ramsay's Authentic Shepherds Pie

Yum. Shepherds pIe always looks liek shit on a plate but tastses good if you make it with lamb proably

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 lbs ground lamb
  • 1 lg Onion
  • 1 lg carrot
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 tbsp worcestershire sauce
  • 1.5 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 sprigs fresh thyme leaves
  • 1 sprig fresh rosemare
  • 1 cup red wine
  • 1 cup chiken stock
  • 2.5lbs potatoes
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1/4 cup cream
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 6 tbsp parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preaheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Heat oil in large pan until hot
  3. Season ground lamb and fry in oil over moderate to high heat for 2-3 minutes
  4. Stir onions and carrot into mince then grate in the garlic
  5. Add worcestershire sauce tomato paste and herbs and cook for 1-2 minutes, stirring constantly
  6. Pour in red wine and reduce until almost evaporated
  7. Add in chicken stock and bring to boil, reduce to simmer until sauce has thickened. Add salt and pepper to taste. Meanwhile cook potatoes in boiling salted water until tender.
  8. Drain potatoes and return to the hot pan over low heat to dry out briefly
  9. Pass them through a potato ricer then beat in the egg yolks, cream and butter, followed by about 2 tbsp grated parmesan. Check for seasoning
  10. Spoon lamb mixture into bottom of ovenproof dish
  11. Using a large spoon layer the mashed potato on top of lamb mixture, starting from outside and working way into the middle
  12. Top with remaining parmesan and season
  13. Fluff up mashed potato with fork to make rough peaks
  14. Bake in oven for approx 20 minutes until bubbling and golden brown
